What's S.E.O?

S.E.O = Search Engine Optimization

This means optimizing your website to appear in Google search results.

Why is S.E.O important?

The higher your website appears in search results, the more likely people are to visit it. This leads to increased traffic, more customers, and higher revenue.

Main Sections of SEO

SEO is divided into three main categories:

First: On-Page SEO

What appears in search results

When you search for a website, search engines typically show:

  • Title (the blue link)
  • Description (the short grey text)

Important factors to appear in the first results

  1. Use relevant keywords in title, URL, and meta description.

  3. Add proper alt text to images: 
    <img src="cat.jpg" alt="Small orange kitten playing with a ball">

Second: Off-Page SEO

What happens outside your website

Search engines consider external factors like:

  • Backlinks: Links from other websites improve authority.
  • Social signals: Shares, likes, and comments show popularity.

How to improve Off-Page SEO

  1. Publish high-quality content others will link to.
  2. Engage on social media to increase visibility.
  3. Build relationships with influencers.

Third: Technical SEO

How well your website is built

Technical SEO improves crawling and indexing. Important factors:

  • Site speed
  • Mobile-friendliness
  • HTTPS security

How to improve Technical SEO

  1. Optimize speed (image compression, caching, minifying code).
  2. Use responsive design.
  3. Implement HTTPS.
